**Insight 1:**
Combat is Highly Concentrated in Specific Zones


Kill events are not evenly distributed across the map — they are heavily clustered in a few areas.


Evidence:
Kill heatmap shows dense clusters in central regions of the map
Multiple matches show repeated combat in the same zones
Low kill activity in outer areas


**Actionable Insight**
- Introduce alternative routes to reduce choke points
- Add cover or obstacles to balance engagements
- Spread high-value loot to other areas to redistribute players


**Impacted Metrics**
- Player survival rate ↑
- Engagement distribution ↑
- Early match elimination rate ↓


**Why a Level Designer should care**


Over-concentrated combat leads to predictable gameplay and frustration. Redistributing fights improves balance and replayability.




**Insight 2**
Bot vs Human Interaction Imbalance
What caught my eye


Bots are frequently involved in combat outcomes(death of the user), impacting player experience.


**Evidence**
-Significant number of BotKilled events, compared to killed(Assumption: "Killed= user_id(player playing a perticular match killed by unknown player ))
-Some areas show higher bot interaction than player vs player combat
-Early match fights often involve bots


**Actionable Insight**
- Adjust bot spawn locations and density
-Balance bot difficulty
- Ensure bots don’t dominate key areas


**Impacted Metrics**
- Player vs Player engagement ↑
- Game fairness perception ↑
- Retention ↑
**Why a Level Designer should care**


Bots should support gameplay, not dominate it. Poor balance can reduce competitiveness and player satisfaction.




**Insight 3:**
Large Portions of the Map are Underutilized


Certain regions of the map show little to no player movement or interaction.


**Evidence**
- Movement heatmap shows very low density in outer zones
- Minimal loot pickup events in these areas
- Players tend to follow similar paths across matches


**Actionable Insight**
- Add loot or objectives in low-traffic areas
- Introduce mission-based incentives to explore
- Improve terrain design to make areas more accessible


**Impacted Metrics**
- Map utilization ↑
- Player exploration ↑
- Session engagement time ↑

**Why a Level Designer should care**


Unused areas represent wasted design effort. Improving utilization makes the map feel richer and more dynamic.
